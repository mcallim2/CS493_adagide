Simple Integrated Development Environment

For C++



SIDE is a software application designed and built in the 49X series at Western Washington University by students, for students. Primarily intended for students in the Computer Science 140 classes, SIDE aims to provide flexibility and power in a controlled environment, making development fast, easy, and even fun!


SIDE is absolutely feature rich, offering you all of the typical functionality of your favorite text editors, while maintaining a clean and simple 'feel'. Find the tools you need, in the places you expect them to be! SIDE also encorporates features such as a file management structure and a reformatting tool that are intended to help new programmers develop good programming practices.


Also, SIDE's user interface (U.I.) was designed with the new programmer in mind. While most developing environments hide useful tools deep inside the menus structure and make them difficult to use, SIDE has built the most useful tools right into the interface for rapid development. Build, run, interact, and stop your program from the bottom half of our specialized UI. What's that? You don't like clicking buttons? We've got you covered - F5, F6, and F7 will build, run and stop (respectively) your program.
